Sadie Veiera Quinn

This website is for Sadie!

Sadie will always be remembered as a kind, loving, curious, and intelligent person. Below are some of the things this website covers.

Travel

Sadie loved traveling - to Brazil to see her family, to the Northeast to write, and to Europe to teach and explore.

Family

Sadie was extremely close with her famly. She loved her parents, aunts, uncles, cousins, and especially her sister.

Books

Sadie absolutely loved reading. She kept track of books in her Goodreads account, where she had read 168 books and had over 200 more books on her "to-read" list.

We love you Sadie.
